HOP ON A TRAIN

Location: Kandy

Prices start from $35

Duration: 7 to 8 hours

Gear up for the most beautiful train journey as your train takes you from Kandy to the scenic, cloud-kissed town of Ella. With such a refreshing experience to offer, it is undoubtedly one of the most unique things to do in Sri Lanka..

Prices start from $66
